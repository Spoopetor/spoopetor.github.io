F1 Blog - Round 19 - COTA

This week's Formula 1 race takes us to the Circuit of The Americas in Austin, Texas. The battle for the championship is staying tight as the season comes to a close.
While Lando Norris is in second place overall, there is still talk of a Verstappen comeback to make it a 5-time Red Bull win streak.
This week's American GP will definitely be an important one to watch in this year's championship battle.
